About Us

Welcome to Infinite Ability

Infinite Ability provides personalized care to help NDIS participants achieve their goals and live fulfilling lives, focusing on safety, skill development, and daily support.

Our mission is to empower individuals with disabilities by creating opportunities and fostering independence. We are committed to respecting your rights and working with you and your support network to help you live the life you choose.

What we do

Our Expertise

Infinite ability provides care and support services for NDIS Participants. We take you out and about to explore your interest and hobbies. We help you to live a positive life.

Home and community support

Infinite Ability helps people with disabilities engage in community activities, building confidence and social connections through tailored support and recreational programs.

Support Coordination

Infinite Ability provides NDIS Support Coordination and Recovery Coaching to help participants achieve their goals and foster independence. Contact us for more information.

Life Skill Development

Infinite Ability provides personal care and life skills support for individuals with disabilities, fostering confidence and independence. Contact us for assistance.

Group, recreation and Leisure

Infinite Ability offers varieties of capacity building, social, creative and active leisure group that can help you find new friendships and learn new skills. We offer Centre based and outdoor activities.

Transport

We are here to provide you with transport facilities as per the NDIS to overcome your daily transportation challenges.

Assist with you independence travel and transport

We assist you to develop the skills you can travel and use public transport independently.

Participation in Education, Training and Employment

We help you to discover your abilities assisting you School, UNI, TAFE and find volunteers or paid job. We assist you to link you in mainstream services.

Short Term Accommodation and Assistance

We Offer Short Term Accommodation and Assistance for NDIS participants as per the need of these individuals and their families. Our short- term accommodation services are a home away from home for people with a disability.

Supported Independent Living (SIL)

We offer Longer term Accommodation and Assistance for Participants. Infinite Ability Provides Supported Independent Living (SIL) supports at Participant’s home or in our facilities.
