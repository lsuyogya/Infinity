NDIS Support Coordination

We provide Support Coordination and Recovery Coaching

We offer comprehensive Support coordination and recovery coaching services for NDIS participants.

Support coordination is a service provided by NDIS registered providers to help NDIS participants achieve their goals and access the best possible services and supports. Support coordinators work closely with participants to understand their needs, strengths, and goals, as well as the funding available under their NDIS plan.

We understand how challenging it can be for individuals with disabilities to navigate the complex world of disability services, which is why we offer expert support and guidance to help participants achieve their goals and aspirations.

Our recovery coaching services are designed to help participants build their resilience and self-confidence while managing their disability. Our experienced coaches work with participants to develop achievable goals and practical strategies for success. Whether it’s developing new skills, increasing social participation, or managing stress and anxiety, our coaches are here to support participants every step of the way.

INFINITE ABILITY

Provides you help and support to meet your need to achieve your goal.

We have qualified staff to provide safe and quality supports to you so that you can live the life the way you want. We offer community inclusion for our participants and recognize the different interest and abilities of all people. The focus is on the Participant meeting their goals, supported by Infinite Ability staff and Participant’s support Network.

We care about you and the important people in your life, so will gladly involve them in planning and decisions about you care according to your wishes.
